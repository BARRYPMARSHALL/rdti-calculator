    ❓ Common Questions

    Will this trigger an ATO audit? ▶
    No. The R&D Tax Incentive is an encouraged program — the ATO and AusIndustry want you to claim it. Over $3B is paid out annually. As long as your claim is documented, there is no elevated audit risk. This calculator gives you an estimate; a registered tax agent handles the actual filing.
    What if I haven't registered with AusIndustry yet? ▶
    You register after the financial year ends but before 30 April. If you spent money on R&D this FY, you can still register and claim. You don't need to pre-register your activities — just document what you built.
    Can I claim for past years? ▶
    Yes — you can amend prior-year tax returns. If you missed claiming for FY24 or FY25, a registered tax agent can lodge an amended return. The 43.5% rate has been stable for years.
    I'm a sole founder with no employees — do I qualify? ▶
    Yes. Your own technical salary, AWS costs, contractor payments, and dev tools all count. Many solo founders get $20K–$80K back. The calculator works for any company size.
